## 🧠 Research Profile

I am a Robotics Engineer focused on the design and control of intelligent mechanical systems for rehabilitation and human assistance.

My current work centers on:

- **Soft Robotics**
- **Shape Memory Alloy (SMA) Actuators**
- **Control systems for rehabilitation exoskeletons**
- **Biomechanical modeling and assistive technologies**

I am academically driven, yet actively seeking new technological challenges in robotics, advanced actuation systems, and intelligent control architectures.
