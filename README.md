### 🚀 About Me

I am a Robotics Engineer and Researcher dedicated to the convergence of technical rigor and humanistic exploration. My work is focused on the preservation of knowledge and the development of technology that impacts society.

- **Engineering:** Specialist in **Rehabilitation, Industrial, and Classical Robotics**. I focus on creating open-source documentation and reproducible knowledge to ensure information remains accessible to all.
- **Cultural Analysis & Literature:** I don't just read; I dissect narratives. From the existentialism of **Kafka’s *Metamorphosis*** to the modern visual storytelling of **Tatsuki Fujimoto's *Chainsaw Man***.
- **Cinematography & Animation:** A deep admirer of world cinema. My influences range from the classics of **Akira Kurosawa** and the atmospheric visuals of **Wong Kar-wai** to the raw intensity of **Park Chan-wook**. In animation, I look up to the visionaries: **Mamoru Oshii**, **Yoshiyuki Tomino**, and **Hideaki Anno**.
- **Current Hobby:** Currently immersed in the craft of **Gunpla** (Gundam plastic models), finding the balance between engineering precision and artistic patience.
